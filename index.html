About us

Hill Top lavender Fields with our organic shop is located in Hill Top (London). Our farm is a popular place during the summer time. You can enjoy lavander fields and make beatiful pictures. The lavender normally starts to bloom towards the middle to end of June and the peak time is July and August however the weather can affect this so be sure to check our website for information or contact us before your visit. During the peak of the season it can get very busy on weekends so we recommend that you plan a week-day visit if possible. We are happy to tell you we have our shop online where you can buy organic laveder products (please see our shop online)
